Description on SmartPollutionWatch

SmartPollutionWatch is a web-based platform designed to predict air pollution levels and provide accessible insights into air quality trends, beginning with Downtown Los Angeles. Developed by team AirSight, the project uses historical air quality data collected via the Environmental API (EAP) to train machine learning models that forecast concentrations of major pollutants and overall AQI.

This website allows users to:

  • View predicted pollutant levels and AQI in a clear, visual format.
  • Explore historical data through interactive tables and charts.
  • Upload their own air quality readings to compare or contribute to model improvement.

The system focuses on practical functionality, combining data-driven forecasting with a user-friendly interface. By enabling both exploration and contribution, SmartPollutionWatch serves as a useful tool for individuals, researchers, and communities who want to stay informed and proactive about environmental conditions.
